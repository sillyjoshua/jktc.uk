About JKTC

JKTC is a YouTube channel built around experimenting with technology and learning through projects.

Content includes Linux systems, networking, self-hosting, game servers, virtualization, web hosting, old hardware, and random homelab ideas.

The channel is mainly about trying things out, seeing what works, breaking stuff occasionally, and sharing the process.

Whether it's building servers, testing software, or setting up strange configurations just for fun, JKTC focuses on hands-on tech experimentation.
